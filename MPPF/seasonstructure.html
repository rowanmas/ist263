MPPF - Season Structure










Structure:

45 game season: each player will play each opponent in one best-of-5 series. 25 of these games will occur before the Spring break, 20 after spring break.
This will be followed by the MPPF post-season.
If a player completes their first 25 games of the season and would like to continue playing, they may do so with leisure games.
These games are friendly and do not affect the season record of players.
